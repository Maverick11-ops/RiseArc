# RiseArc: The Nemotron-Powered Financial Guardian
RiseArc is a next-generation financial app powered by NVIDIA's Nemotron-3-Nano. It simulates personalized risk scenarios based on real-time news, delivers financial analysis based on the scenarios, and generates actionable plans for the user. 
RiseArc is *not* just a chatbot. It's a full-stack artificial intelligence designed to deeper understand, protect, and strengthen their financial future. 


## Our Philosophy
With the rapid rise of AI, new jobs and opportunities are emerging, but that also means numerous jobs being replaced. A lot of people may lose their only way of putting food on their tables. That's what RiseArc was built to prevent; to protect people from financial instability. 


## Key Features
All key features are powered by NVIDIA's Nemotron-3-Nano.

### Scenario Engine 
Simulates realistic "what-if" situations:
- job loss
- inflation spikes
- emergeny expenses
- income cut
- etc
  
Each scenario produces a full financial impact analysis and prevention strategy.

### Survival Timeline
Calculates how long a user can sustain their lifestyle based on:
- income
- expenses
- savings
- other factors

Outputs a clear month‑by‑month projection.

### Guardian Mode 
Automatically detects financial risks such as:
- high burn rate
- low emergency reserves
- overspending
- unstable income
  
Then generates actionable recommendations.

### Nemotron Powered Reasoning
Uses NVIDIA Nemotron-3-Nano to: 
- interpret user input
- decide which custom simulation tools to call
- generate structed insights
- explain results in human readable language

### Real Time Financial Analysis
Produces: 
- risk scores
- spending breakdowns
- resilience metrics
- recommended adjustments
- and much more
